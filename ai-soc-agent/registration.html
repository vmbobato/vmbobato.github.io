What clients will do

The onboarding process is designed to be simple for non-technical business owners and small teams.

1. Share your website setup

Tell me where your website runs, whether you use AWS or NGINX today, and what kind of activity you want help reviewing.

2. Confirm your logs

We identify which AWS and NGINX logs your website already creates and how they can be safely submitted for review.

3. Review the findings

When early access is available, submitted logs are reviewed and turned into clear findings and reports.

What early access includes

AI SOC Agent is focused on visibility and reporting. It does not automatically change your website or fix issues by itself.

Secure log submission

Clients will submit website logs through a secure API and receive review status and downloadable reports.

Readable security findings

The product highlights suspicious activity, adds useful IP context, and explains the findings in plain language.

Help understanding results

I can help review the findings with you and discuss practical next steps to better secure your website.

No codebase access required

Clients do not need to see or manage the codebase. The focus is on submitting logs and understanding the report.
